Supported Analytics Providers

Examples of compatible tracking services

Google Analytics

Most popular analytics platform. Get your tracking code from Google Analytics > Admin > Tracking Info.

Matomo (Piwik)

Open-source, privacy-focused alternative. Self-hosted or cloud option available.

Plausible Analytics

Lightweight, privacy-friendly analytics. No cookies, GDPR compliant.

Fathom Analytics

Simple, privacy-focused analytics without cookie banners.

GoatCounter

Free, open-source web analytics platform focused on privacy.

Custom

Any JavaScript-based tracking code will work. Just paste the entire snippet.
